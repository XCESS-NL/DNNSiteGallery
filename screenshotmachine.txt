Screen Shot Generator Service
-----------------------------
https://www.screenshotmachine.com

API URL Format:

http://api.screenshotmachine.com/?key=[key]&size=[size]&format=[format]&hash=[hash]&cacheLimit=[cacheLimit]&timeout=[timeout]&url=[url]

Example:

http://api.screenshotmachine.com/?key=1234xx&size=X&format=JPG&cacheLimit=0&timeout=0&url=[URL]

( you need to register and substitute your own key )
